Col Jonathan Williams

Father of the Corps of Engineers

This is a time line of Jonathan Williams life

  • 1750 -Williams was born in Boston in the Province of Massachusetts Bay and attended Harvard University.
  • 1770 to 1785 -He spent most of this period in England and France.
  • 1787 -He returned to the United States.
  • 1801 -He was appointed a Major in the Corps of Artillerists and Engineers by John Adams.
  • 1802-He was appointed Superintendent of West Point
  • 1807 to 1811 Williams designed and complted construction of Castle Williams, in New York.
  • 1812-He resigned from the Army and was elected to congress
  • 1814 -He died of gout before the assembly of Congress and was buried in Philidelphia.
